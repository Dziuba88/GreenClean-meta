Upholstery & Leather Cleaning

Upholstery is one of the most important aspects of the lifestyle your own home affords you. Your furniture, recliners, chairs, and sofas add value to your life and they require proper care and cleaning every now and then so they keep beautiful and their useful life is protracted. We will do that for you in the Dublin, Kildare and Meath area.

How do we clean your upholstery?

We know how to clean all kinds of upholstery. We use a variety of upholstery steam cleaning methods.

The Eco Clean Solution's professional technicians are uniquely trained to treat every kind of fabric used in upholstery (including the most exotic ones). Polycotton, polyester, brocades, berber, velour, velvet, linen, cotton. Any kind. We know how to deal with it and clean it without any damage or discoloring.
